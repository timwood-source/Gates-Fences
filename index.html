Pembroke Hill School

Visiting Campus

The safety of our students, faculty and staff is our highest priority. Pembroke Hill is committed to maintaining a welcoming environment while providing consistent and secure access to both campuses. These procedures apply to visits during regular school days and hours.

All visitors, including parents, guardians, family members, guests, vendors and other individuals conducting business on campus, must check in at an approved visitor location before proceeding to any destination.

All visitors must present a valid government-issued photo ID and will be screened through the CENTEGIX Visitor Management Platform. Once approved, visitors will receive a visitor badge, which must be worn visibly at all times while on campus.

Wornall Campus

During school hours, all visitors to the Wornall Campus must check in at the Security Kiosk located at the Wornall Main Gate, regardless of their destination. Visitors may not enter through side gates or proceed directly to a building unless specifically directed by Security or authorized school personnel.

Ward Parkway Campus

During school hours, visitors to the Ward Parkway Campus must check in at the Upper School Office, Middle School Office, or Security Operations Center at the Bellis Athletic Center. Visitors should use the location most appropriate for their destination and may not proceed elsewhere without first checking in.

Visitor Expectations

  • Enter through an approved visitor entrance.
  • Present a valid government-issued photo ID.
  • Complete the required screening and check-in process.
  • Wear the issued visitor badge visibly.
  • Remain in approved areas associated with the visit.
  • Follow directions from Security personnel, faculty and staff.
  • Check out and return the visitor badge when leaving.

Procedures may be modified for events, performances, athletics, summer operations, weather, or temporary security conditions. Alternate instructions will be communicated by the school.

Pembroke Hill School reserves the right to deny, restrict or revoke campus access when necessary to protect the safety, security and orderly operation of the school community.
